## About Hodlr Swiss

Hodlr Swiss is a Swiss-based brand specializing in secure physical cryptocurrency storage solutions. We focus on providing high-quality metal seed phrase storage products to help users protect their digital assets.

Our educational content aims to empower cryptocurrency users with the knowledge they need to securely manage their own assets.
